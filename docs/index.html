About

Polycat Girl is a mix of 1/1 and generative PFP project with 5,555 supply under Polygon Blockchain. It has an all-female character, including wide range of fashion styles, keeping up with its value to represent everyone.

The collection aims to spread equal love to the community which concept revolves around the dress-up madness and creativity of the main character, Poly.

The collection is founded and designed by Eul Arts.
